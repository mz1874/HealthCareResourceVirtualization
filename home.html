Introduction to Health Resources

Health resources are key indicators of the quality and accessibility of medical services within society. The following points outline major aspects of health resource development.

  • Medical Facilities: Covers the number and distribution of medical facilities.
  • Medical Staff: The growth in the number of healthcare professionals.
  • Health Service Coverage: Emphasizes the importance of expanding health service coverage.
